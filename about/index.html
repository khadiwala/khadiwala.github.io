About

Ravi Khadiwala

Ravi Khadiwala is a recent graduate of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a desire to solve problems using his unique multidisciplinary perspective. His academic interests gravitate around Math, Computer Science, and Economics, and he’s interested in subjects like AI, Machine Learning, Algorithms, Distributed Systems, Graph Theory, and Game Theory. He will graduate with two degrees, one in Computer Science and the other in Math and Economics. He enjoys longboarding, comic books, and well made television shows, as well as over analyzing cartoons obviously made for children. Ravi has seen the entire run of “The Wire” 4 times, and is not ashamed to admit it. If anything, he probably says it too much. He owes a significant portion of his upbringing to watching Simpsons reruns. Ravi is currently located in the Chicago area.
